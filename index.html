What is Hult Prize UCEK?

The Hult Prize is a global student competition that challenges young people to solve pressing social issues through innovative and entrepreneurial solutions. The UCEK chapter aims to foster social entrepreneurship and provide students with a platform to showcase their ideas. The UCEK chapter plans to train students and help them develope their ideas. We also plan to conduct events and workshop for the students.
